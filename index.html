Fort Tryon Park

Fort Tryon Park is located in the bustling city of New York. Hidden away inside this beautiful park, is one of The City's Metropolitan Museums of Art.

The Museum lives only at two iconic sites in New York City; Fifth Avenue and The Cloisters in Fort Tryon. The MET presents thousands of years of art from around the globe for anyone to enjoy. Photo By: Yuri De La Rosa, Fort Tryon Park The Cloisters, MET Museum The Met has always gone above and beyond to be a treasury of rare and beautiful objects. Each day, art comes alive in the Cloister's galleries and through its exhibitions and events, revealing new ideas and unexpected connections across time and across cultures.
